ElectroTech by Deb

Site Name

ElectroTech by Deb – This name represents my personal portfolio showcasing both my web development projects and Electrical/Electronics engineering projects. It is simple, memorable, and reflects my technical skills and brand identity.

Site Purpose

The website serves as a personal portfolio and professional showcase. It provides:

  • Information about my skills and background in Web Development and Electrical/Electronics Engineering.
  • Showcase of completed projects with descriptions and images.
  • A contact form for potential clients or collaborators to reach me.

Scenarios

Questions from a visitor representing the target audience:

  • How can I view Deborah’s completed web development projects?
  • How do I contact Deborah for a project or collaboration?

Color Schema

The site uses the following primary colors:

  • Primary: #003366 – Used for headings, logo accents, and footer background.
  • Secondary: #f4f4f4 – Used for page background and section backgrounds.
  • Accent: #004d4d – Used for buttons, links, and highlights.

Typography

Selected fonts for the website:

  • 'Bebas Neue' – Used for headings (projects, page titles).
  • 'Open Sans' – Used for body text and paragraphs.
  • 'Roboto' – Used for form input and labels for readability.
